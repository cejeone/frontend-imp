About Inovamap

Established in January 2012, PT. Inovasi Mandiri Pratama (Inovamap) is a geospatial technology-based company. In addition, we are also strong in the application of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) in terms of software procurement, strategic solutions and GIS-based application development
